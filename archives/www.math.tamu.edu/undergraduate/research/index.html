Research with mathematics faculty

  • If you have any questions about mathematics undergraduate research at Texas A&M University, please contact Michael Anshelevich. Many of the Mathematics professors work with undergraduates on research projects, so students should also feel free to contact them directly. For brief descriptions of their research interests, see the list of
    • Mathematics undergraduate research mentors
